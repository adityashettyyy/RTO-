Our Services

Everything You Need for License Registration

Our portal simplifies the process of applying for a Learner's License (LL) or Permanent Driving License (DL) in your state. Easily fill forms, upload documents, and get status updates in real-time.

We also offer services like license renewal, duplicate license issue, address update, and test slot booking.

Learn the Process

Registration Process

Step 1: Online Application

Choose your license type, fill the application, and upload documents.

Step 2: Slot Booking

Select your test center and book a convenient date & time for the Learner/Driving test.

Step 3: Appear for Test

Visit the center and complete the theoretical or practical driving test.

Step 4: License Issuance

Track your status online and download/receive your approved license.

Documents Required

For Learner's License

  • Age Proof (Birth Certificate, 10th Marksheet)
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar, Passport, Utility Bill)
  • Passport-size Photograph

For Driving License

  • Valid Learner's License
  • Form 4 (Application for DL)
  • Test Certificate (issued by RTO after passing)
