About RTO Priority Services Portal

Learn about our commitment to simplifying regional transport services with transparency, speed, and accessibility.

Our Journey

Established in 2025, the RTO Priority Services Portal was launched to streamline access to essential transport services. Designed with user convenience at its core, the portal connects citizens directly with Regional Transport Offices for efficient and transparent service delivery.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless and user-friendly digital platform for availing key RTO services — reducing waiting times, eliminating paperwork hassles, and ensuring a smooth experience for every citizen.

Our Vision

To revolutionize transport governance by enabling real-time access to priority RTO services through innovative digital solutions and inclusive service models.

What We Offer

  • Online application and tracking for driving licenses
  • Vehicle registration and ownership transfer services
  • Smart scheduling for driving tests and appointments
  • Real-time status updates via SMS/email
  • Dedicated support for senior citizens and differently-abled individuals
  • Secure payment gateway for all government fees
