Our Services

Simplifying Ownership Transfers

This portal allows you to transfer vehicle ownership easily — whether selling, inheriting, or gifting a vehicle. Complete the formalities online and avoid long queues.

Also access services like RC update, insurance transfer assistance, address change, and NOC tracking.

Learn the Process

Ownership Transfer Process

Step 1: Submit Application

Choose transfer type, enter vehicle details, and submit the online form.

Step 2: Upload Documents

Upload RC, insurance, Form 29 & 30, ID proof, and NOC (if needed).

Step 3: Verification

RTO verifies details and may request physical verification of vehicle.

Step 4: RC Transfer

New RC is issued in the buyer’s name and delivered digitally or via post.

Documents Required

For Ownership Transfer

  • Original Registration Certificate (RC)
  • Form 29 & Form 30 (duly filled)
  • Valid Insurance Certificate
  • Valid Pollution Under Control (PUC)
  • Government-issued ID Proof (Both Seller & Buyer)
  • No Objection Certificate (if vehicle is from another RTO)
