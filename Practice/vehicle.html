Our Services

Everything You Need for Vehicle Registration

Our portal helps you register your new vehicle easily and efficiently. Submit forms, upload documents, and receive real-time status updates.

We also provide services like ownership transfer, address change, RC renewal, and duplicate RC issuance.

Learn the Process

Registration Process

Step 1: Online Application

Select vehicle type, fill the application, and upload required documents.

Step 2: Fee Payment

Pay the applicable registration fee securely through the portal.

Step 3: Vehicle Inspection

Visit the RTO for physical inspection and verification of documents.

Step 4: RC Issuance

Track your application status and download your Registration Certificate (RC).

Documents Required

For New Vehicle Registration

  • Form 20 (Application for Registration)
  • Invoice from Dealer
  • Valid Insurance Certificate
  • Proof of Address (Aadhaar, Passport, Utility Bill)
  • Photograph & ID Proof

For Ownership Transfer

  • Form 29 & 30 (Transfer Forms)
  • Original RC
  • Valid Insurance
  • Pollution Under Control (PUC) Certificate
